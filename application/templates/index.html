The Intergenerational Alliance was formed to foster bottom-up dialogue between young and old, rich and poor, and across genders on various matters affecting Nigerian society and its state. Intergenerational Alliance forums bring together small groups of Nigerians from across the generational divide, from all walks of life, religious backgrounds, and ethnicities, to talk about the past, present, and future of our home, Nigeria. We believe in conversation. We believe in genuine and complex communication that bridges division, increases understanding, and unites us in communal purpose. We believe that for the benefit of our future, it is essential to dialogue.

ENIOLA

Dr. Ẹniọlá Ànúolúwapọ́ Ṣóyẹmí is a political theorist whose academic research specialises in theories and practices of deliberation, the nature of freedom, and the nature of legitimate authority. Over the last 10 years, Ẹniọlá's work has focused on making the historical and contemporary socio-legal and political experiences of Nigeria, and the wider African continent, influential to mainstream discourses in political theory. Her field research has involved working in Lagos, Nigeria in partnership with LAMATA

Prior to entering academia, she worked at Chatham House and at the UK Guardian's Lobby Office in the House of Commons. She holds a Ph.D. in Political Science from Boston University and an MSc. in International Public Policy from University College London. She has recently been a Max Weber Fellow and a Visiting Fellow at the European University Institute.

OYINKAN

Oyinkan Akintola-Bello is a public interest technologist who works at the intersection of governance and technology. With an undergraduate education from Bristol University in Law and a Master’s degree in Conflict Conflict, Security and Development from the University of Sussex her expertise spans working with governments to innovate and improve service delivery to supporting startups with advisory on policy decisions

For her it is important that citizens have a voice in how their affairs are managed, this informs the work she does around data privacy and digital rights
